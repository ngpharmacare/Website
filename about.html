About Us

Pioneering excellence in pharmaceutical and cosmetic innovation

Our Story

Founded over 15 years ago, NG Pharma & Care has grown from a small research laboratory into a leading provider of pharmaceutical and cosmetic solutions. Our journey has been defined by an unwavering commitment to scientific excellence and innovation.

We believe that the future of healthcare and wellness lies in the integration of rigorous scientific research with cutting-edge technology. This philosophy has guided us through numerous breakthrough discoveries and successful product launches.

Today, we serve clients across the globe, from emerging startups to established pharmaceutical companies, helping them bring safe, effective products to market.

Our Mission

To advance human health and wellness through innovative pharmaceutical and cosmetic solutions, grounded in rigorous scientific research and ethical practices.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a trusted partner in pharmaceutical and cosmetic development, setting new standards for quality, innovation, and scientific excellence.

Our Values

Scientific integrity, quality excellence, innovation, ethical responsibility, and collaborative partnership guide everything we do.
