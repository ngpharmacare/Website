Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Research projects typically take 6-12 months, while manufacturing and regulatory consulting projects can range from 3-18 months. Contact us for a specific timeline estimate.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we serve clients globally and have experience with regulatory requirements in multiple countries including the US, EU, and Asia-Pacific regions.

What certifications do you hold?

We are GMP certified, ISO 9001:2015 compliant, FDA registered, and EMA compliant. Our facilities undergo regular inspections and audits.

Can you help with product development from concept to market?

Absolutely. We offer end-to-end services covering all stages from initial research and formulation development through manufacturing, regulatory approval, and market launch.

What is your minimum order quantity for manufacturing?

Minimum order quantities vary by product type. We can accommodate pilot batches as small as 5,000 units and scale up to full commercial production. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How do I request a quote or consultation?

Simply fill out the contact form above or reach out to us directly via email or phone. We'll schedule an initial consultation to discuss your project requirements and provide a detailed proposal.
